The first ever Pythonista and Pythoneer

A brief timeline of Guido van Rossum

  • 31 January 1956: Guido van Rossum is born in Haarlem, Netherlands.
  • 1982: Guido van Rossum earns his master 's degree in Mathematics and Computer Science from the University of Amsterdam
  • 1982: Guido van Rossum joins CWI as a researcher.
  • 1990: Python is released at CWI.
  • 1991 to 1995: Guido van Rossum worked for the multimedia group at CWI, Netherlands.
  • April 1995 - February 1998: Guido van Rossum joins U.S. National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) as a guest researcher.
  • 2000: Guido van Rossum marries Kim Knapp.
  • October 2000 - July 2003: Guido van Rossum joins Zope Corporation.
  • July 2003 - December 2005: Guido van Rossum joins Elemental Security.
  • 2006 - December 2012: Guido van Rossum joins Google.
  • January 2003: Guido van Rossum joins Dropbox.
  • April 2018: Guido van Rossum becomes a Computer History Museum Fellow.
  • October 2019: Guido van Rossum retires officially.
